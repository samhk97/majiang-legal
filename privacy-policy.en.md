# Privacy Policy | Mahjong Club

> **Effective Date**: 2026-05-06
> **Applies to**: "Mahjong Club" mobile app (iOS / Android)

Mahjong Club (the "App", "we") respects your privacy. This policy explains **what we collect, why, how we handle it, and how you can control it**.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Automatic
- **Anonymous Device ID**: The App uses anonymous device-based login. The server generates a UUID tied to your device as your user ID. We **do not collect** your Apple ID, Google account, email, name, or phone number.
- **Game data**: club memberships, in-game chip balance, match history, chip ledger, diamond ledger. Stored on our servers to provide core gameplay.
- **Connection data**: IP address (used only for connection, abuse prevention, and regional restriction; not retained long-term), session tokens.

### 1.2 Provided By You
- **Club name, invite code, table settings** when you create a club or configure a room.
- **Support messages** when you contact us.

### 1.3 Payment Information (Third Party)
- Diamond In-App Purchases are processed by **Apple App Store / Google Play**. We **do not receive** your credit card or payment details. We receive only the **receipt** from Apple/Google to verify purchase authenticity.

---

## 2. How We Use Information

| Purpose | Data |
|---|---|
| Core gameplay (matches, settlement, chat) | Device ID, game data |
| Anti-cheat and multi-account prevention | Device ID, IP, session tokens |
| Process IAP and grant diamonds | IAP receipts |
| Customer support and dispute resolution | Support messages, related match logs |
| Aggregate analytics (**anonymized**) | Gameplay statistics (not linked to individuals) |

We **do not** use your data for:
- Advertising or marketing push (the App contains **no third-party ad SDKs**)
- Sale or rental to any third party
- Sharing with data brokers

---

## 3. Nature of Chips and Diamonds (Important)

- **Chips are an in-game scoring unit for entertainment only. They have no cash value and cannot be redeemed.**
- Players **may not** exchange chips for real currency or any asset of real value.
- The App **does not provide** chip withdrawal, transfer, gifting, or any form of redemption.
- **Diamonds are an in-app purchase currency** used only for opening tables by club hosts. **Non-refundable, non-transferable, non-redeemable for cash** (except per Apple / Google refund policies).

---

## 4. Data Retention and Deletion

- Your account data is retained until you **actively request deletion**.
- You may request deletion via in-app **Settings → Account → Delete Account** (in a future version). **Deletion is permanent and irreversible**, including: club memberships, chip balances, match history.
- For accounting and AML compliance, **diamond IAP transaction records** are retained for **5 years** (anonymized).
- If you don't log in for 30 days and uninstall the App, we invalidate your session token (but retain account data until deletion).

---

## 5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We disclose your data **only**:

1. **Under legal compulsion**: pursuant to a lawful order of judicial or administrative authority.
2. **Fraud / cheating detection**: with other club hosts (limited to their club's scope) when sharing minimal abnormal-play records is necessary.
3. **Service providers**: our self-hosted Nakama backend (data stored on cloud VPS directly managed by us); Apple / Google for IAP.

We **do not** share data with any other third party.

---

## 6. Security

- Connections use **HTTPS / WSS (TLS)** encryption.
- Your session token is stored in the device's AsyncStorage (system-encrypted storage).
- We do our best but cannot guarantee absolute security. If you suspect unauthorized account access, contact support immediately.

---

## 7. Children's Privacy

This App is **not intended for users under 17 years of age** (includes mahjong-style content; no real-money transactions). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 17. If you discover a minor using the App, please notify us so we can delete the account.

---

## 8. Regional Restrictions

This App is **not available to users in mainland China**. The App may also be unavailable in certain jurisdictions due to local regulations.
